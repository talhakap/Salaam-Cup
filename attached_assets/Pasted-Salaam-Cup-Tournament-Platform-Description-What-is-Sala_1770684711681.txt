Salaam Cup – Tournament Platform Description
What is Salaam Cup?

Salaam Cup is a multi-tournament sports competition platform designed to manage community-based tournaments from registration all the way to verified rosters. It is built to support multiple tournaments running under one brand, each with its own divisions, teams, and players, while keeping the process organized, fair, and scalable.

The platform’s core purpose is to:

Collect team registrations

Ensure teams are approved before competing

Collect player rosters in a structured way

Verify player eligibility

Clearly identify missing or unregistered players

Keep everything organized for tournament administrators

Who uses it?

Team Captains

Register their team for a specific tournament and division

Submit a roster of players for their team

Receive confirmation and approval updates by email

Players

Are listed on team rosters

Must be properly registered and eligible to play

Admins

Review and approve teams

Review rosters and player eligibility

See which players or teams are missing required steps

Manage multiple tournaments from one system

Core Tournament Flow
1. Tournament Setup

Each tournament exists as its own entity inside Salaam Cup.
A tournament has:

A name

One or more divisions

Registration status (open or closed)

Its own teams and rosters

Multiple tournaments must be able to exist at the same time without interfering with each other.

2. Team Registration (Captain Flow)

A team captain selects a tournament and division

Submits team details and their contact information

After submission:

The team is not immediately active

The team is marked as pending approval

The captain receives a confirmation message saying the team was received and is under review

3. Admin Approval

Admins review pending team registrations

Admin can approve or reject a team

Once approved:

The team becomes active

The captain is notified that their team is approved

The captain is invited to submit their roster

No team can submit a final roster or participate unless approved.

4. Roster Submission

The approved team captain submits a roster containing multiple players

Each player submission includes:

Full name

Date of birth

Phone number

Email address

A single roster submission may include many players at once

5. Roster Review & Validation

Once a roster is submitted, the system separates players into two states:

Roster Staging

Newly submitted players live here first

Each player is checked against existing registered players

Players are flagged if:

They have not registered previously

Required information is missing

Details do not match an existing record

Roster Final

Players who meet all requirements are moved here

These players are considered eligible to compete

Admins must be able to clearly see:

Which players are verified

Which players are missing steps

Why a player is blocked

6. Unregistered / Missing Players

The system must clearly show:

Players who appear on a roster but have not completed registration

Players who are missing required steps

Teams with incomplete rosters

This is critical so admins and captains know exactly what needs to be fixed before game day.

7. Communication & Notifications

The platform should automatically communicate:

Team registration received

Team approved or rejected

Roster received

Roster issues (missing or unregistered players)

Communication must be clear and timely so captains are never confused about their status.

Multi-Tournament Requirements

Salaam Cup must:

Support many tournaments over time

Keep tournaments isolated from each other

Allow new tournaments to be added without disrupting existing ones

Maintain historical data from past tournaments

Admin Experience

Admins need:

A clear dashboard

Lists of teams by tournament and approval status

Roster views showing staging vs final

Filters for missing players and issues

Simple actions (approve, reject, flag)

What Success Looks Like

Captains always know what stage they’re in

Admins always know what’s missing

No eligible player is missed

No ineligible player slips through

Adding a new tournament feels simple and repeatable
